Simon Porte Jacquemus

Simon Porte Jacquemus is the founder of the fashion label Jaquemus. He was born on January 16, 1990 in Salon-de-Provence, France. In 2008, at the age of 18, he went to Paris, where he studied at the École supérieure des arts et techniques de la mode for a short time. When his mother died, he began his own career as a fashion designer at the age of 20.

Jacquemus Digitized.

This website is meant to serve as a digital representation of the Jacquemus Fall/Winter 2020 & Spring/Summer 2021 collections. Simon was able to create beautiful shows in the midst of the pandemic and this site serves as a way to immerse yourself in the collections as if you were sitting front row.
